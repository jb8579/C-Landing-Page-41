Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

At Tatts Lotto, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website (tatts-lotto.au). This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them. By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to improve the performance of websites, provide a better user experience, and remember user preferences for future visits.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Enhancing the functionality of our website, such as remembering your preferences.
  • Analyzing website traffic to improve the user experience.
  • Personalizing your experience and delivering targeted content and advertisements.
  • Ensuring that our services function properly and securely.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website and cannot be turned off.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us analyze how visitors use our website, enabling us to improve its performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your choices (such as language preferences) to improve your browsing experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver content and advertisements that are relevant to you, based on your interests.

5. Managing Cookies

You have the ability to control and manage cookies in your browser settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies. Please note that rejecting some cookies may affect your experience on our website and the services we provide.

6. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to use cookies on our website. These third parties may collect data about your online behavior across various websites. We have no control over these cookies, and their use is governed by the privacy and cookie policies of the third parties involved.

7. Changes to Our Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Cookie Policy at any time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
